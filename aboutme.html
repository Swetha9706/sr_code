About Me

S
Swetha · Pune, IN

I design things that feel good to use — and even better to look at.

Hi, I'm Swetha — a UX researcher, UI designer, and graphic designer based in Pune. I'm drawn to the messy, human side of design: the part where you sit with real people, understand what frustrates them, and turn those insights into interfaces that just click.

I care equally about the big picture (how does this product make people feel?) and the small details (is that button actually 8px too wide?). My work spans digital products, brand identity, and editorial design.

Outside of work you'll find me sketching in Muji notebooks, obsessing over type specimens, and making very strong opinions about colour contrast ratios.
